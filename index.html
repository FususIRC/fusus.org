FususIRC

FususIRC is an IRC network created with the purpose of introducing teens and young adults into computing whilst providing an environment that shows no judgement towards any user.

Connections

FususIRC servers accept connections via the round robin irc.fusus.org. IPv6-only connections may utilize IPv6.fusus.org to connect.

Plaintext connections are accepted globally using ports 6667-6669. SSL connections are accepted on port +6697 globally.
