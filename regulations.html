FususIRC Charter

To ensure a productive environment, FususIRC has enacted a charter that must be adhered to. If a client has a disagreement with a topic discussing in the charter, they are asked to disconnect from FususIRC.


- All clients connecting to FususIRC must be 13 years of age or older due to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998.

- All clients connecting to FususIRC are subject to a scan of their machine to detect proxies.


Forbidden Activities

- Distribution of child pornography.

- Stalking or harrassment of any user or staff member.

- Impersonation of any user connected to FususIRC

- Spam and/or flooding of a user or channel.

- Distribution of copyright-protected property.

- Creation of sex-related channels.
